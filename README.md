## 🛠 About pua

pua is a Windows application designed to help you monitor and improve your AI agent’s skills and progress. It provides clear tracking tools to assist engineers who face performance improvement plans (PIPs). Whether you are trying to meet specific development goals or follow up on assigned tasks, pua will help you organize your journey in a simple way.

This tool targets users who want a straightforward way to view the status of their AI agents, keep records of improvements, and stay on top of deadlines.
